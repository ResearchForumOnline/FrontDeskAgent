# Roadmap

FrontDeskAgent is intended to be a useful self-hosted front desk system, not just a demo chatbot.

## Near Term

- Cleaner first-run setup wizard.
- More guided industry templates.
- Better OpenZero connection checks.
- Stronger website ingestion controls.
- More integration examples for n8n, Zapier, Make, CRMs, booking tools, and call/SMS providers.
- More Voicebox examples for self-hosted staff alerts and desk-side speech.
- More screenshots and videos for non-technical users.

## Mid Term

- Playbook import/export from `FrontDeskAgent-Playbooks`.
- Better lead scoring and duplicate detection.
- More appointment workflow controls.
- Optional staff approval queues.
- More polished business dashboard views.
- Easier deployment behind FreeWebPanel or other self-hosted panels.

## Long Term

- Multi-location business support.
- Better voice-call orchestration and richer Voicebox call-flow examples.
- More local model profiles for CPU-only servers.
- Richer OpenZero supervision workflows.
- Privacy-first analytics.
- Commercial support and managed deployment paths outside the public repository.

## Principles

- Self-hosted first.
- CPU-friendly first.
- Useful without paid AI keys.
- Hosted APIs optional, not mandatory.
- Customer data belongs with the operator.
- Public repo stays open-source; private customer deployments keep their private data outside Git.
